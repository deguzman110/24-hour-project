Synopsis

The best intentions often come back to haunt you. MISSION: IMPOSSIBLE - FALLOUT finds Ethan Hunt (Tom Cruise) and his IMF team (Alec Baldwin, Simon Pegg, Ving Rhames) along with some familiar allies (Rebecca Ferguson, Michelle Monaghan) in a race against time after a mission gone wrong. Henry Cavill, Angela Bassett, and Vanessa Kirby also join the dynamic case with filmmaker Christopher McQuarrie returning to the helm.

DIRECTED BY

Christopher McQuarrie

CAST

Tom Cruise, Alec Baldwin, Simon Pegg, Rebecca Ferguson, Ving Rhames, Michelle Monoghan, Angela bassett, Vanessa Kirby

CHARACTERS

ETHAN HUNT

Actor: Tom Cruise

An IMF agent on the run after failing to capture Solomon Lane during a mission.

AUGUST WALKER

Actor: Henry Cavill

A CIA assassin working for the Special Activities Division.

LUTHER STICKELL

Actor: Ving Rhames

An IMF agent and member of Hunt's team.

BENJI DUNN

Actor: Simon Pegg

An IMF technical field agent and member of Hunt's team.

ILSA FAUST

Actress: Rebecca Ferguson

A former MI6 agent who joined Hunt's team previously.

SOLOMON LANE

Actor: Sean Harris

An anarchist mastermind and the leader of the Syndicate.

ERICA SLOANE

Actress: Angela Bassett

The new Director of the Central Intelligence Agency, and Walker's superior.

WHITE WIDOW

Actress: Vanessa Kirby

A black market arms dealer.

ALAN HUNLEY

Actor: Alec Baldwin

Former CIA Director who is currently the IMF Secretary.
